## Setting off from Crowden

After finding the car park, making use of the 'facilities' and exploring the campsite for potential ice cream options on our return we set off up the Penine Way out of Crowden towards Laddow Rocks. The path snakes its way west out of Crowden along a rocky path before heading north.
